Hardware

Purpose-built sensors

Two sensor form factors designed for different deployment scenarios.

paxed puck

Zone occupancy sensor

Lateral mounting at wall or waist height. Scans across open areas to estimate how many people are present. Perfect for canteens, reading halls, and waiting areas.

  • 2D LiDAR (Xiaomi-style rotation scanner)
  • ESP32 Wi-Fi CSI sensing
  • Wi-Fi + BLE density estimation
  • Outputs: count, density level, trend

paxed perch

Entrance flow sensor

Top-down mounting above doorways and corridors. Tracks people crossing a boundary line to determine entry and exit direction, plus flow rate.

  • 3D LiDAR (top-down depth sensing)
  • Radar (velocity + motion cues)
  • ESP32 Wi-Fi CSI + BLE density
  • Outputs: entries, exits, net flow, rate
